Programs

Programming for Every Member of Your Family

From young children to elders, from scholars to first-time seekers — JAINA Convention 2027 is designed for the whole community.

Spiritual Learning

Keynotes, workshops, and guided sessions with leading Jain scholars, sadhus, and sadhvis — connecting ancient wisdom to modern life.

  • Keynote addresses by leading scholars
  • Daily temple puja & prayers
  • Meditation & mindfulness sessions
  • Interfaith dialogue panels

Youth & NextGen

A full dedicated track for Jain youth — built to challenge, connect, and inspire the next generation of community leaders.

  • Pathshala competitions & academic bowls
  • Breakouts with engaging speakers
  • Cultural performances & talent showcase
  • Networking & mentorship circles

Evening Programs

Three spectacular evenings celebrating Jain culture through music, dance, drama, and storytelling — each one a highlight of the convention.

  • Friday: Welcome messages & garba
  • Saturday: Live Drama event
  • Sunday: 4th of July celebration & Bollywood Night Extravaganza

Breakout Tracks

50+ curated sessions across parallel tracks covering spirituality, science, health, business, environment, and Jain identity in America.

  • Professional & entrepreneurship track
  • Jainism & science symposium
  • Environmental activism & ahimsa
  • Health, wellness & Jain diet

Family Experience

Dedicated programming ensuring every family member — from toddlers through grandparents — feels included and part of the JAINA community.

  • Kid's club activities
  • Family wellness & lifestyle workshops
  • Jain vegetarian & vegan food experiences
  • Community service opportunities

Grand Finale

A grand finale bringing the full JAINA community together — celebrating accomplishments, honoring service, and looking ahead.

  • Community recognition & awards
  • Youth achievement celebration
  • JAINA leadership address
  • Passing of the torch ceremony
