Sponsorship That Builds the Convention

“In Jain convention tradition, a labh is an auspicious opportunity to participate in and earn the spiritual merit of a sacred activity.”

The JAINA Convention is the premier biennial gathering of the North American Jain community — the single largest gathering of Jains outside of India. Held every two years since 1981, it brings together Jains from across the United States and Canada for spiritual programs, youth events, scholarly sessions, cultural celebrations, and community building.

The 2027 Dallas Convention will be hosted at the Hilton Anatole over the July 4th weekend. Your sponsorship makes this gathering possible, and every dollar goes directly into programming that uplifts our community and strengthens Jain Dharma in North America. JAINA is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit — all sponsorships are fully tax-deductible.
